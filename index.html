Bio

Dragons Delight began in 2014, as owner Saima Awan started baking cakes from her new home in Amsterdam. In 2015, Dragons Delight found its lair in the Tolstraat, and opened for breakfast and brunch in July 2015. Joined by hospitality veterans, Jade Ashworth in 2017 and Maggie De Bruin in 2019, they have taken Dragons Delight to the next level. The lair is now showcasing both seasonal menus as well as new and established artists- the mantra of Dragons is to never stop creating and growing.
