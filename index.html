Bio

Dragons Delight began in 2014, as owner Saima Awan started baking cakes from her new home in Amsterdam. In 2015, Dragons Delight found its lair in the Tolstraat, and opened for breakfast and brunch in July 2015. Joined by hospitality veterans, Jade Ashworth in 2017 and Maggie De Bruin in 2019, they have taken Dragons Delight to the next level. The lair is now showcasing both seasonal menus as well as new and established artists- the mantra of Dragons is to never stop creating and growing.

Very pleased and will return!

Find Us

Tolstraat 200, Amsterdam 1074VN, Nederland.
You can find us at:
Tolstraat 200, Amsterdam 1074 VN, The Netherlands.
To get here from Amsterdam Centraal:

You can take Tram 3 or 4 to Van Woustraat, and turn left down Tolstraat and walk to the end.

Alternative Transport Methods:

Amsterdam is of course the city of bikes, so you could also cycle to us with a trusty satnav system and lock your bikes up outside. Uber and Viavan also operate in the city but you will need their apps to book one!
